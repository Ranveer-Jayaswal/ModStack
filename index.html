The Founder
Built by a car person,
for car people.

I'm Ranveer — I'm 18 years old and I've been modifying cars since I could drive. I built ModStack because I got tired of losing track of everything I'd done to my 2004 Honda CRV. Receipts in notes apps, costs in my head, mod list nowhere. When someone asked what I had done, I couldn't properly answer.

ModStack is the tool I wished existed. A permanent, shareable record of every modification — so your build has proof, not just a story. We're pre-revenue, growing by word of mouth, and every feature is free while we build the community.

About ModStack
The verification standard
for modified cars.

ModStack is a vehicle provenance registry built for the modified car community. We give every builder a permanent, shareable digital record of their build — replacing verbal claims with documented proof.

Think of it as CarFax, but for modifications. Instead of ownership history, we track the modification history — every part installed, every dollar spent, every upgrade planned.
